Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Skince is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. In short: we don't require an account, we don't sell your data, and we don't use it to train models.

What we handle

To provide the app's core feature, the following is handled:

  • User Content (Photos or Videos) — not linked to you; not used for tracking; purpose: App Functionality.
Ingredient-label photos are sent to the backend LLM only to analyze the request, then processed and not retained. The skin-concern profile + saved shelf stay on-device. No account, no tracking.

Third-party processing

When you use an AI feature, your input is sent securely to our processing service (and its AI provider) solely to produce your result. It is processed in the request and not retained, and is never used to train models.

Your choices

You control what you submit. On-device data can be deleted at any time from within the app or by removing the app.

Children

Skince is not directed to children under 13.
